🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

1. Information Collection and Use

DinoAlarm is committed to protecting your privacy. We want to assure you that:

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties (except as noted below for ads)

2. Data Storage

All alarm data (time, dinosaur sound selection, labels, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. Permissions

DinoAlarm requires the following permissions to function:

  • Notifications: To trigger alarms at scheduled times
  • Audio Playback: To play dinosaur sounds
  • Background Execution: To ensure alarms work even when the app is closed

4. Advertising

The free version of DinoAlarm displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad personalization purposes. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Information that may be collected by AdMob includes:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

Note: Premium users (who purchase the ad-free version) will not see any advertisements and will not be subject to AdMob data collection.

5. In-App Purchases

DinoAlarm offers in-app purchases for premium features. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple's App Store and are subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not collect or store any payment information.

6. Video Content

The anti-snooze video feature (premium only) loads videos from external sources (Google Drive). When accessing these videos, you may be subject to Google's privacy policies. No personal viewing data is collected by DinoAlarm.

7. Children's Privacy

DinoAlarm is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we don't collect any personal information at all, children can safely use this app.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using device passcodes and keeping your device updated.

10. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete all alarms within the app at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will my alarms work if I close the app?

A: Yes! DinoAlarm uses iOS notifications to ensure alarms trigger even when the app is closed.

Q: Why isn't my alarm ringing?

A: Please check the following:

  • Notifications are enabled for DinoAlarm in iOS Settings
  • Your device volume is turned up
  • Do Not Disturb mode is disabled (or alarms are allowed)
  • The alarm is enabled (toggle is ON)

Q: Can I get a refund for my premium purchase?

A: All purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund, please visit Apple's Report a Problem page.

Q: How do I delete my data?

A: Simply delete all alarms within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data. All data is stored locally on your device only.

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes! We don't collect or transmit any personal data. All alarm data stays on your device.

Q: Can I use DinoAlarm offline?

A: Yes! All core alarm features work offline. Only the anti-snooze videos require an internet connection (premium feature).
